Amherst College's Women in Computer Science Club

As president of the WiCS Club, I created a website to provide information about our mission and events throughout the school year. The site also allows those who are interested join our mailing list. MEME SEARCH-INATOR

During my summer at Google's Computer Science Summer Institute, I worked with two other participants to create a website that works as a search engine for memes. I designed and developed the internal social network for users. I also worked in the backend to allow users to register, login, and logout using their existing Google accounts.

Classroom Anonymous

My current and ongoing project is an educational tool that makes it easier for students to speak up about their comfortability with newly learned topics. A typical scenario would begin with the teacher asking their students, "Have you understood everything in today's class?" Their students would then press thumbs up or thumbs down on their devices to reflect their confidence, and the teacher is provided with the anonymous classroom responses.
